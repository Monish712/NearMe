Naidupet - My home town

Naidupeta is a municipality and the headquarters of a mandal in Andhra Pradesh's Nellore district, with a population of over 45,000 people as of the 2011 census. It is a developing town that faces issues like traffic congestion, parking problems, and pollution, but is also considered an important industrial and transport hub, with residents advocating for better rail connectivity to cities like Chennai. The town has a local government body led by a chairperson and commissioner.
